Modifications And Refunds

Modifying/Cancelling your booking

If you need to make changes to your reservation, we recommend that you go to the airline's official website or mobile phone app. You may also have Airline Travel Support do it for you.

Getting a refund

You may be able to get a refund or a partial refund in the event of cancellation. Depending on the type of flight ticket you have booked, you will get its entire cost refunded to the original payment method, or it will be credited to your airline account as flight points. We highly recommend you to contact Airline Travel Support, where real experts deal with customer service agents. We will do our best to secure a refund during our communications with the airline.

Baggage Information

As you may already know, most airlines have a free hand luggage policy that allows passengers to take a small briefcase, purse, laptop or backpack on board, free of charge. Please get in touch with our Airline Travel Support for more information regarding baggage policies.

Onboard Services

There are plenty of onboard services such as meals and flight entertainment like movies, magazines and wi-fi available to all airline passengers for free. These onboard services may differ from one airline to another, so contact us immediately to find out what will be available to you on your flight.

Flight Delay And Cancellation

It can rarely happen for a flight to be cancelled or delayed. However, in such cases, airline companies may book you onto the next available flight or accommodate you in another way. Even though this is extremely rare, it is not impossible. Most airlines will usually provide their passengers with a full refund or some compensation for the inconvenience.

Boarding Process

Usually, passengers are divided into groups based on their flight ticket number. Always stay alert for announcements when boarding and follow the instructions when your group is called for a smooth transition from airport to onboard.

Documentation Required

Do not forget to bring your government-issued photo ID with you when you show your flight ticket. You will need it to be confirmed by the airport security staff and by the airline at the check-in counter so that you can receive a boarding pass. The only ID that will be accepted for international flights at the airport is your passport. In case you are travelling with a newborn, do not forget to bring their birth certificate as it will serve as their only valid form of ID.
